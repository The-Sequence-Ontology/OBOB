Welcome to OBOB: the OBO browser.
OBOB is a generic browser for ontologies that uses OBO format.
  OBOB offer a variety of features including:

* Graphical views of a term's place within the ontology and links to its is_a parents/children.

* Searching based on terms or synonyms.

* Visual exploration of ontology and terms using a expandable, cascading tree.

* Display detailed information about a term, its definition and relationships.

* Export of terms and relationships in multiple formats.

* OBOB was developed by the Sequence Ontology via a grant from the NHGRI (R01HG004341).
